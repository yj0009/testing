About Us

Built on experience, delivered on time

Commercial Landscape Australia is a Melbourne-based landscaping contractor. We handle landscape design, construction and maintenance for education, aged care, residential and commercial property sites — taking on projects of any size, from a single garden refresh to a full grounds build.

Every project is handled by our own crew from start to finish. The team that designs the space is connected to the team that builds it, and the team that keeps it looking good long after handover. No subcontractor hand-offs, no lost details — one contractor, accountable for the whole job.

We work with property managers, builders, school operations teams and facility operators who need a landscaping contractor that turns up on time, communicates clearly and stands behind the quality of their work.

What We Do

Our services

Three core services, applied across all the sectors we work in. The same crew handles each phase — design, build and ongoing maintenance.

01 — DESIGN

Landscape Design

We start with a site visit and a clear brief, then develop a design that fits the budget, the site and the people who use it — practical plans ready for sign-off and build.

  • Site assessment & design brief
  • Concept & detailed plans
  • Planting & softscape layouts
  • Paving, walls & hard surface design
  • Irrigation & drainage layout
02 — CONSTRUCTION

Landscape Construction

Once a design is locked in, our crews build it — coordinating all materials and sequencing on site so the job finishes on time and to spec.

  • Turf supply & installation
  • Garden bed & planting installation
  • Paving, paths & hard surfaces
  • Retaining walls
  • Irrigation system installation
03 — MAINTENANCE

Landscape Maintenance

Ongoing grounds care on a schedule that suits the site — keeping it looking presentable, safe and healthy through every season without fuss.

  • Mowing, edging & lawn care
  • Pruning, weeding & garden upkeep
  • Irrigation checks & adjustments
  • Seasonal clean-ups
  • Regular site reports

FAQ

Common questions

Answers to questions we're often asked by clients looking for landscaping services across Melbourne.

What areas do you service?
We service Melbourne Metro, working on education, aged care, residential and commercial property sites throughout the region.
What landscaping services do you offer?
We offer three core services: landscape design, landscape construction and landscape maintenance. This covers everything from turf and planting to paving, retaining walls and irrigation systems.
Do you work on small projects or only large commercial sites?
Both. We take on jobs of all sizes — from a single garden bed refresh or lawn care visit through to a full grounds construction and long-term maintenance contract.
Can you provide ongoing maintenance after construction?
Yes — many of our clients move straight from a design and construction project into a regular maintenance schedule. It's easier for everyone when the same crew looks after the site from start to ongoing.
How do I get a quote?
Call or WhatsApp us on +61 456 396 193, or fill in the request form on this page. We'll get back to you promptly to arrange a site visit and quote.
Do you work in schools and aged care facilities?
Yes — education and aged care are two of our four core sectors. We understand the access, safety and compliance requirements that come with these environments and plan accordingly.
Are your crews insured?
Yes, our crews are fully insured and operate under strict on-site safety standards on every project, regardless of site type or scale.
